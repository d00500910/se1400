The Iconic Game

Super Mario Bros, a game known to all. From its first version in the early arcade days to modern-day gaming, it has been a staple in the gaming world. It has always made the best party games to enjoy with friends and offers so many experiences. If you want a racing game, Mario has it. If you want a 2D game, Mario has it. If you want an open-world experience, Mario has it! From casual gameplay to competitive speedruns, it is a game anyone can play and love!

Where it Began

Making his first appearance in the original Donkey Kong in 1981, Mario was the hero of his story, saving his princess. Over the years, he evolved with the times while always maintaining his charm. He introduced a universe filled with memorable characters and a kingdom to protect, expanding beyond just the screen. The game has always been a fan favorite and a cherished treasure to many.
